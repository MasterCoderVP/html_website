My top 3 movies of all-time

Gifted

A heartfelt drama that beautifully explores family dynamics and the balance between nurturing talent and protecting a child's childhood.

Shawshank Redemption

A deeply emotional and uplifting story of hope and friendship within the confines of prison.

Gone Girl

A twisted, gripping thriller that explores the dark complexities of marriage, media manipulation and deception.
